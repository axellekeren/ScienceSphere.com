What is sains?

Science is a human effort to understand the universe and all the phenomena that occur in it through observation, experimentation and rational thinking. The goal of science is to expand human knowledge, understand the principles that govern the universe, and apply that knowledge to solve problems, improve technology, and improve the quality of life. Science covers various fields such as

physics, chemistry, biology, mathematics, computer science, and many more.
